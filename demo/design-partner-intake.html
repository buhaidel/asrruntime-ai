ASR Design Partner Intake

Ideal Design Partner
Teams operating autonomous workflows, AI agents, orchestration pipelines, or operational automation systems.
Primary Problems
  • Workflow failures are difficult to reconstruct.
  • Operational incidents lack replay evidence.
  • Recovery coordination is fragmented.
  • Runtime trust visibility is limited.
ASR Demonstrates
  • Replay reconstruction
  • Mutation simulation
  • Recovery orchestration
  • Runtime telemetry
  • Operational evidence generation
Validation Goal
Determine whether replay-driven operational trust improves incident understanding, recovery visibility, and workflow auditability.
