ASR Discovery Call Structure

1. Workflow Pain Discovery
Understand how the team currently investigates workflow failures, debugging pain, replay gaps, and operational blind spots.
2. Current Runtime Stack
Identify orchestration frameworks, runtime tooling, observability stack, and persistence model.
3. Replay Reconstruction Discussion
Explore whether replay-oriented evidence and runtime continuity would improve incident understanding and recovery confidence.
4. Trust & Audit Concerns
Capture concerns around verification, replay correctness, operational trust, and evidence credibility.
5. Next-Step Qualification
Determine whether a technical walkthrough, design-partner discussion, or replay artifact review is appropriate.
