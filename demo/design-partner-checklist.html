ASR Design Partner Checklist

Target Partner
Teams operating AI workflows, autonomous agents, workflow automation, or runtime orchestration systems.
Qualification Criteria
  • Has real workflow failures or debugging pain.
  • Needs better incident reconstruction.
  • Can share sample logs, traces, or failure scenarios.
  • Understands auditability or operational trust value.
  • Accepts design-partner scope, not production SLA.
Demo Sequence
  • Open Runtime Portal.
  • Show Replay Evidence.
  • Show Mutation and Recovery.
  • Show Runtime Events.
  • Show Incident Center.
  • Show Replay Export Package.
  • Show Verification Engine.
Do Not Promise
  • No production SLA.
  • No legal compliance certification.
  • No unlimited support.
  • No custom integration without written scope.
  • No token, crypto, or settlement claims in first validation.
Success Signal
The design partner says ASR would help them understand, reconstruct, or govern workflow failures better than their current process.
